About Cooking With Karly


Hello everyone, welcome to Cooking with Karly! My name is Karly Anderson, and if you like cooking, and eating good food, then you have come to the right place! I enjoy helping people living with special needs (and their families) learn important life skills while achieving more independence in the kitchen.

I offer small group and private in-home cooking classes so children and adults living with special needs can create delicious and healthy meals in a fun and inclusive environment.

My goal is to create a friendly and inclusive community for students, parents and guardians by providing hands on cooking classes, as well as, educational resources for both children and adults living with special needs, and their families.
